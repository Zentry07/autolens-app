Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

AutoLens ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the AutoLens mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Photos and Images: When you use AutoLens to scan a car, the photos you take or upload are sent to our AI processing service (Anthropic) for vehicle identification and analysis. These images are processed in real-time and are not stored on our servers after analysis is complete.

VIN Numbers: If you use the VIN decoder feature, the VIN you enter is sent to our AI service for analysis. VINs are not stored after processing.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience, such as which features are used most frequently. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Purchase Information: If you subscribe to AutoLens Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide vehicle identification and analysis services
  • To process and manage your subscription
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To provide customer support

3. Third-Party Services

Anthropic (AI Processing): Car photos and queries are sent to Anthropic's API for AI-powered analysis. Anthropic processes this data according to their privacy policy and does not use your data to train their models. Images are not retained after processing.

RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat processes subscription data according to their privacy policy.

Apple: All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Apple's privacy policy governs how your payment data is handled.

4. Data Storage

AutoLens stores your scan history, favorites, and preferences locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any server. You can delete all local data at any time through the Settings page in the app.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in this policy (AI processing for core functionality).

6. Data Retention

Photos and queries sent for AI analysis are processed in real-time and are not stored after the analysis is complete. Local data on your device is retained until you delete it through the app's Settings.

7. Children's Privacy

AutoLens is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all your locally stored data through the app's Settings
  • Revoke AI processing consent at any time through Settings
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
